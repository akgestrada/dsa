Hiroshima Internation Science Fair

The Leadership and Exchange Programs Committee would want to invite you to apply as a student-participant during the Exchange program with students from Hiroshima, Japan. We are looking for twenty-five students who would like to participate in the said program. The applicant should/must:

  • be 15 to 17 years old;
  • have NO pending disciplinary case;
  • have good communication skills in English;
  • be in Good Standing last school year (SY 2022-2023);
  • have NO grade lower than 2.50 in any of the subjects in the First Quarter, SY 2023-2024; and
  • preferably have interest in Japanese culture and with talents in music and/or dance (for the group performance).

Additionally, all students who will be selected for the program are expected to participate in the events in school on January 5 and 7, 2024 (Friday 9-11AM and Sunday 830AM-430PM, respectively).

Moreover, seven (7) out of all the participants in the program will be chosen to join the Intramuros Tour with the Japanese delegates on January 6, 2024 (Saturday).

If you are interested to apply, please fill out this form by 26 Dec, 12NN. Some FAQs will be posted on this link.
